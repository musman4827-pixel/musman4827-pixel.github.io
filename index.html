The FreshFlow Advantage

The Nose Strip Effect: Maximum Intake

Our nose strips use Spring-Flex technology to gently lift the sides of the nose. This widens the nasal valve—the narrowest part of your airway.

The Result: You get up to 50% more oxygen per breath. This reduces the effort needed to breathe during sleep (stopping snoring) and keeps your heart rate lower during exercise for better stamina.

The Mouth Strip Effect: Deep Recovery

Breathing through your mouth at night dries out your throat and triggers "fight or flight" stress responses. Our mouth strips use a gentle, cross-shaped tension to remind your jaw to stay closed.

The Result: You force your body to use its natural filtration system (the nose). This humidifies the air, boosts nitric oxide production, and ensures you stay in deep REM sleep longer without waking up parched or groggy.
